## Installation

The app will soon be available via a **Closed Android test**. 
Access to the test will be granted via a questionnaire that will allow you to join the test and then install the app from the Play Store. 
The app will then be available on the Play Store.

## Description

Silvernote is a modern, fast, and secure note-taking app.
Organize your ideas with folders and tags, across all your devices.
